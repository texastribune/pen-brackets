The 2016 Texas Primaries

By Madlin Mekelburg and Ryan Murphy | Last Updated: Dec. 15, 2015

  • Tweet
  • Recommend
  • Email

The ballots for 2016’s Republican and Democratic primary elections are set and in many cases, the primary races are expected to be more competitive than the general election races in November. This is an unofficial list of who filed to run in the election. The parties are finishing up their work and getting official versions to the state; we will update and finish our list as they finish theirs. Incumbents' names are in parentheses.
